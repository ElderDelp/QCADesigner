Creating Cells

QCADesigner provides several methods for adding cells to your design.

Cells of two different sizes may be present on your design. Their physical parameters (such as overall cell size, dot spacing, dot radius, etc.) can be set via the "Cell Properties" dialog available from the "Edit" menu:
