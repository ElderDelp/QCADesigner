GTK+ Workaround Widgets

GTK+ widgets need to be worked around for two reasons:

  • because they lack functionality advertised by their respective documentation, such as failing to issue "notify::" signals when one of their properties changes.
  • because needed functionality is only available in GTK+ versions later than the one QCADesigner currently depends on and needs to be backported, such as the missing "sensitive" property for GtkCellRenderer.
